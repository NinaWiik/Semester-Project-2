How to play

Choose a character and and the "play game" button i enabled. Roll the dice on the left side by clicking on the dice. Watch out for the dragon traps, they will make you go back some tiles. You win the game when you reach the "Finish" tile. You need to get the exact number on the dice to get to the finish line. When this tile is reached, you have won the game.
